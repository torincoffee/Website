Client:
Journey Into The Woods

Role:
Design + Branding

I recently wrote and illustrated a children's book called "Journey into the Woods," which tells the story of two brothers on a mission to collect ingredients for their mother's special Sunday soup. As a graphic designer, I wanted to bring the story to life in a new and exciting way. Therefore, I developed a concept for a website version of the book.

The website version of "Journey into the Woods" allows readers to interact with the story in a dynamic and engaging way. Each page of the book is brought to life with interactive elements, including animations and sound effects. The website also features a variety of fun and educational activities related to the story, such as coloring pages and recipe ideas.
My goal with the website was to create a truly immersive experience for young readers. By bringing the story to life in this way, I hope to inspire a love of reading and creativity in children.
