Ingredients:

  • 4-5 fresh limes, washed and quartered
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 cups cold water
  • ½ cup sweetened condensed milk
  • Ice

Instructions: Start by placing the quartered limes and sugar in a blender. Blend until the limes are completely crushed, and the mixture is juicy and fragrant. Add 3 cups of cold water and blend again for a few seconds. Pour the mixture through a fine mesh strainer, using a spatula to press out as much juice as possible. Discard any solids that are left in the strainer. Pour the sweetened lime juice into a pitcher. Stir in the sweetened condensed milk until it is well incorporated. Add ice to the pitcher and stir again. Serve the Brazilian Lemonade chilled in tall glasses, garnished with a slice of lime.

Enjoy your delicious and refreshing Brazilian Lemonade!
